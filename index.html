Capability Statement

One perspective across financial, technology, and operational risk.

Our work is designed for organizations that require clear criteria, documented evidence, disciplined analysis, and recommendations that can be implemented.

Interglobe LLC provides financial consulting, information technology risk, cybersecurity, audit, compliance, and internal control services to government agencies, government contractors, and regulated organizations. We help clients strengthen financial accountability, improve technology governance, address cybersecurity and compliance risks, evaluate control effectiveness, and develop practical solutions for complex operational challenges. Our capabilities include financial audit support, audit readiness, compliance risk management, financial process improvement, information system auditing, IT general controls reviews, third-party technology risk, cloud governance, data governance, business continuity, and operational resilience.

Interglobe is led by a Certified Public Accountant and experienced technology risk professional with regulatory experience at the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency. Our leadership holds CPA, CISA, CISM, and CRISC credentials and brings experience across financial audit, cybersecurity, information systems, regulatory compliance, governance, risk management, and internal controls within highly regulated environments. Our work is grounded in defined criteria, documented evidence, risk-based analysis, and clear communication, with a focus on identifying root causes, prioritizing material risks, and delivering practical recommendations that improve accountability, resilience, compliance, and mission performance.
