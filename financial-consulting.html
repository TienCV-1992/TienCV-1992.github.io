Audit support

Financial Audit and Audit Readiness

Interglobe supports organizations before, during, and after financial audits. We help management evaluate documentation, organize supporting evidence, assess control effectiveness, identify audit risks, and address deficiencies before they become repeat findings.

Our services may include:

  • Audit-readiness assessments
  • Prepared-by-client support
  • Supporting-documentation reviews
  • Control testing and evidence validation
  • Audit-request coordination
  • Finding and recommendation tracking
  • Corrective-action planning
  • Remediation validation
  • Repeat-finding prevention
  • Management response development
02

Control effectiveness

Internal Controls and Risk Assessments

Effective internal controls must do more than exist on paper. They must be properly designed, consistently implemented, supported by evidence, and monitored over time.

Interglobe evaluates financial and operational controls to determine whether they appropriately address material risks. We examine governance, roles and responsibilities, segregation of duties, approvals, reconciliations, access controls, documentation, monitoring, escalation, and management reporting.

Our work helps organizations:

  • Identify control gaps
  • Clarify control ownership
  • Strengthen accountability
  • Reduce financial and operational risk
  • Improve the quality of supporting evidence
  • Establish sustainable monitoring processes
  • Prioritize remediation based on impact and urgency

Obligations & oversight

Compliance Risk Management

Interglobe helps organizations understand and manage obligations arising from laws, regulations, policies, contracts, grants, and internal requirements.

Our compliance services may include:

  • Compliance risk assessments
  • Regulatory-gap analyses
  • Policy and procedure reviews
  • Compliance-control mapping
  • Monitoring-program design
  • Issue-management support
  • Corrective-action development
  • Compliance reporting
  • Management and board-level reporting
  • Quality-assurance reviews

We focus on whether compliance programs are capable of identifying risk, assigning accountability, detecting problems, escalating issues, and producing timely corrective action.

04

Efficiency with control

Financial Process Improvement

Financial processes frequently develop through years of incremental changes, manual workarounds, fragmented systems, and unclear ownership. These conditions can increase cost, reduce transparency, and create control weaknesses.

Interglobe reviews end-to-end financial processes to identify:

  • Unnecessary manual activity
  • Duplicate reviews
  • Control gaps
  • Process bottlenecks
  • Unclear responsibilities
  • Data-quality problems
  • Ineffective reporting
  • Opportunities for standardization or automation

We develop practical recommendations that balance efficiency, control, compliance, and operational realities.

Our support may include:

  • Management dashboards

  • Budget-to-actual reporting
  • Issue-aging analysis

Prevention & program integrity

Fraud Risk and Program Integrity

Fraud risk cannot be managed exclusively through after-the-fact investigations. Organizations need preventive controls, risk indicators, effective monitoring, and clear escalation procedures.

Interglobe assists clients with:

  • Fraud-risk assessments
  • Control-environment reviews
  • High-risk process identification
  • Red-flag development
  • Monitoring and reporting frameworks
  • Segregation-of-duties reviews
  • Corrective-action planning
  • Program-integrity assessments
