Information Technology, Cybersecurity and Risk Services

Protecting Mission-Critical Systems Through Stronger Governance, Controls and Resilience

Government organizations depend on information systems to manage operations, protect sensitive information, deliver public services, support financial activities, and execute their missions.

Interglobe helps government organizations identify technology risks, evaluate cybersecurity and IT controls, strengthen governance, improve resilience, and develop actionable remediation strategies.

Our approach

Risk-based technology assurance grounded in mission requirements.

As technology environments become more interconnected, agencies must address cybersecurity threats, legacy systems, cloud adoption, third-party dependencies, data risks, regulatory requirements, and rapidly evolving operational challenges.

Our approach combines information-system auditing, cybersecurity management, regulatory experience, financial-control knowledge, and enterprise risk management.

02

Cybersecurity leadership & oversight

Cybersecurity Governance and Program Assessments

Cybersecurity requires more than technical tools. Effective programs depend on leadership, accountability, policies, skilled personnel, risk management, monitoring, incident response, and continuous improvement.

Interglobe evaluates cybersecurity programs to help organizations understand:

  • Whether cybersecurity responsibilities are clearly assigned
  • Whether risks are identified and prioritized
  • Whether policies and standards are current
  • Whether controls are consistently implemented
  • Whether management receives meaningful reporting
  • Whether issues are remediated effectively
  • Whether cybersecurity is integrated into enterprise decision-making

Our cybersecurity consulting services may include:

  • Cybersecurity program assessments
  • Governance reviews
  • Framework-gap assessments
  • Policy and standards development
  • Cybersecurity risk assessments
  • Corrective-action planning
  • Security-control documentation
  • Management reporting
  • Cybersecurity metrics and dashboards
  • Remediation validation
